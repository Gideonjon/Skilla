Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access Skilla on my mobile device?

Simply download the Skilla mobile app from your device's app store, sign in with your account, and start learning on-the-go, anytime, anywhere.

Can I switch between devices while using Skilla?

Absolutely! Skilla is designed for seamless multi-device learning. Your progress is synchronized across devices, allowing you to transition effortlessly from your mobile to your computer without missing a beat.

Are the courses on Skilla compatible with various mobile operating systems?

Yes, Skilla supports both iOS and Android platforms. Our mobile app is optimized to deliver an excellent learning experience regardless of your device's operating system.

How can I track my progress and achievements on the Skilla app?

Skilla provides a user-friendly dashboard where you can easily monitor your progress, view completed courses, and track achievements. The app ensures you stay motivated and informed about your learning journey.

Are there offline learning options available on the Skilla app?

Yes, Skilla understands the importance of flexibility. Users can download selected courses for offline access, enabling learning even without an internet connection. Perfect for those moments when you're on the move.

Can I interact with instructors or other learners through the Skilla mobile app?

Certainly! Skilla fosters a sense of community. Engage in discussions, participate in forums, and even attend live sessions through the mobile app. Connect with instructors and fellow learners to enhance your learning experience.
